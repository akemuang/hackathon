About the Challenge

The challenge provides a structured opportunity for students to work with real organizational and business problem contexts, select an appropriate technical approach, and deliver a functional project with measurable outcomes.

Core AI Tracks

Teams may focus on one track or integrate multiple tracks where it strengthens real-world applicability.

1) Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Modern organizations generate large volumes of text (messages, reports, reviews, emails, policies). NLP enables systems to analyze and generate language for actionable insight and intelligent interaction.

  • Text classification and sentiment analysis
  • Chatbots and conversational systems
  • Information extraction and summarization
  • Translation or question-answering systems

2) Data Visualization

Visualization helps organizations communicate insight clearly, support analysis, and make evidence-based decisions through well-designed visuals.

  • Exploratory and explanatory visualization
  • Data storytelling and narrative visualization
  • Interactive/static analytics
  • Complex or temporal data representation

3) Object Detection

Object detection identifies, locates, and classifies objects in images/video for domains such as security, healthcare, retail, logistics, and smart cities.

  • Image/video-based detection
  • Real-time systems
  • Smart city and safety applications
  • IoT and automation integration

Expected Outcomes

End-to-end functional prototype

Demonstrate a complete workflow from data to AI processing to user-facing output.

Organizational value

Clear benefits to enterprises such as efficiency, automation, decision support, or safety.

Measurable results

Evidence-based metrics including model performance or operational improvements.

Responsible AI readiness

Considerations of privacy, ethics, transparency, and deployment feasibility.
